About

I'm a Senior UI/UX Designer with strong front-end development skills and nine years of experience leading digital projects from design to implementation. I'm driven to create intuitive and engaging experiences that align user needs with business objectives, consistently delivering impactful and accessible designs.

How I approach design

My design journey began as a kid with pen and paper. To me, visual design—whether for print or digital—is like drawing. It depends on space, color, hierarchy, and contrast.

This has always felt natural and fuels my creativity. But I wanted to go further. So, I learned UX and front-end development to expand my skills and create more impactful digital experiences—from concept to launch.

Now, I understand the full design lifecycle, from gathering requirements to working in agile teams. And I use my skills to keep on playing and experiementing with design, and now code.
