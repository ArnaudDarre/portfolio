About

I am Senior UI/UX Designer with a strong knowledge of Front-end Developement and 9 years of experience leading digital projects from design to implementation. I am passionate about creating and enhancing digital products that are user-friendly, accessible, engaging, and functional.

How I approach design

Design, whether for print or digital medium, is not so different from a drawing. It all has to do with the use of breathing space around elements, colours, hierarchy and contrast.

This is why each design should start with a pen and paper. This playful but effective method enhances creativity by shortening the gap between the brain and the design tool, your hand.
