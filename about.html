About

With over eight years of experience, I am passionate about creating and enhancing digital products and platforms that are user-friendly, engaging, and functional. I have a strong background in branding and UI/UX design, as well as front-end development skills that allow me to bring my designs to life with code.

I like to build complex, coherent design systems and layouts on a broad set of mediums. I am particularly interested in typography and its use in printed and digital media, and I decided early on in my career to learn coding to broaden my set of skills and enhance my design work.

How I approach design

Graphic design, whether for print or digital medium, are not so different from a drawing composition. It all has to do with the use of breathing space around elements, colours, hierarchy and contrast.

This is why each design should start with a pen and paper. This playful but effective method enhances creativity by shortening the gap between the brain and the design tool, your hand.
