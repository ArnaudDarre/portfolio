How I approach design:

Want to draw something together?

Graphic and even web design are not so different from a drawing or a painting composition. It all has to do with the use of breathing space around elements, colours, hierarchy and last but not least contrast.

This is why each design should start with a pen and paper. This playful but effective method allows to enhance creativity by shortening the bond between the brain and the design tool: your hand (and not the computer).
